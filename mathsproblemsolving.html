Combinations and permutations

Firstly, we must establish the definition of n!, which just means n*(n-1)*(n-2)*...*1. Combinations are expressed as nCr, which means n choose r. This is choosing r objects or values from n objects or values. nCr is equal to n!/r!(n-r)!. Permutations are expressed as nPr, which is equal to n!/(n‚àír)!.
